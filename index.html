I am a PhD student at School of Computer Science of Carnegie Mellon University, adviced by Prof. Shubham Tulsiani. I am a part of CMU Computer Vision Group. I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science from School of EECS of Peking University, where I was fortunate to be adviced by Prof. Liwei Wang. I also spent some wonderful time working with Dr. Han Hu at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) and Prof. Xiaolong Wang at UCSD.

My research interests are computer vision, computer graphics and machine learning, especially 3D vision, scene understanding and label-efficient learning.

I am always open to research collaborations. Feel free to contact me.
