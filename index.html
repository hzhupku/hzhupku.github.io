I am a PhD student at School of Computer Science of Carnegie Mellon University, adviced by Prof. Shubham Tulsiani. I am a part of CMU Computer Vision Group. I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science from School of EECS of Peking University, adviced by Prof. Liwei Wang. Prior to PKU, I got my Bachelor's degree in Physics from Nanjing University and worked at LAMDA of NJU led by Prof. Zhihua Zhou. I spent a wonderful summer in 2021 doing internship at UCSD, adviced by Prof. Xiaolong Wang.

My research interests are Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Especially, I am interested in 3D Vision, Scene Understanding and Label-Efficient Learning.

Here is my resume. You can also view my Google Scholar profile.
